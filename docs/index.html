事情都不在它该在的地方
Nothing Is Where It Should Be
一间看起来秩序井然的房间——五行各占一面墙，方位分明。但佛像藏在镜子里，香灰本该喂鱼，打火机遗落在书桌上。越是按照"规则"行事，越觉得荒谬。恐怖不在黑暗中，而在秩序的缝隙里。
A room that looks orderly — each element on its wall, directions clear. But the Buddha is hidden inside a mirror, incense ash belongs in the fish tank, the lighter sits forgotten on a desk. The more you follow the "rules," the more absurd it feels. Horror hides not in darkness, but in the cracks of order.
概念
CONCEPTS
五行·归位·民俗恐怖
Five Elements · Restoration · Folk Horror
一个中式审美的密室空间，四面墙和一面地板分别对应木火金水土。看似完美的五行排列，实则佛像错位、风水冲突、角落里闪过不该出现的影子。玩家需要把所有东西归位——砸碎镜子取出佛像、把佛像放回佛龛、喂香灰给鱼、点燃香炉——直到房间恢复它该有的样子。
A Chinese-aesthetic escape room. Four walls and one floor map to Wood, Fire, Metal, Water, Earth. A seemingly perfect five-element arrangement, but the Buddha is misplaced, feng shui is broken, and shadows flash where they shouldn't. The player must restore everything — smash the mirror to retrieve the Buddha, return it to the shrine, feed ash to the fish, light the incense — until the room becomes what it was meant to be.

核心机制
CORE MECHANICS
五行归位密室探索
Five-Element Restoration Room Exploration
玩家与四面墙和一面地板交互（金木水火土），发现道具，然后主动打破错误、把一切归位：砸碎镜子取出佛像 → 放回佛龛 → 喂香灰给鱼 → 点香炉 → 占卜问卦。房间不是被"等待"打开的，是被"修复"后才允许离开的。
Players interact with 4 walls and 1 floor (Metal, Wood, Water, Fire, Earth), discover items, then actively break what's wrong and restore what's misplaced: smash mirror → retrieve Buddha → return to shrine → feed ash to fish → light incense → divine with cup. The room opens not by waiting, but by being restored.

VISUAL STYLE
中式手绘密室
Chinese-Hand-Drawn Chamber
所有墙面和道具均为手绘美术，色调深沉温暖，笔触带有中国传统绘画质感。每面墙都有多张状态图（如镜子碎裂四阶段、佛龛点燃前后、鱼缸喂鱼变色等）。
All walls and props are hand-drawn art with deep warm tones and traditional Chinese brushwork texture. Each wall has multiple state images (mirror shattering in 4 stages, shrine before/after lighting, fish tank color shift, etc.).

文化层次
CULTURAL LAYERS
五行·风水·民俗恐怖
Five Elements · Feng Shui · Folk Horror
五行相克：金克木，木克土，土克水，水克火，火克金。这个循环构成了房间的秩序表面。
风水冲突：镜子对着佛龛——看似合理的五行排列，暗藏违和。荒谬就藏在秩序底下。
民俗恐怖：角落闪过的暗影、镜子深处藏着佛像、喂鱼时水面变色的那一刻——恐怖不在鬼怪本身，在于"不该出现的东西出现在了秩序里"。
Five-Element Cycle: Metal overcomes Wood, Wood overcomes Earth, Earth overcomes Water, Water overcomes Fire, Fire overcomes Metal. This cycle forms the room's orderly surface.
Feng Shui Conflict: The mirror faces the shrine — a seemingly proper arrangement harbors disharmony. Absurdity hides beneath order.
Folk Horror: Shadows flashing in corners, a Buddha statue hidden deep inside a mirror, the moment the water changes color when you feed the fish — horror is not the ghost itself, but "something that shouldn't be here, appearing within order."
叙事设计
NARRATIVE DESIGN
从秩序到安宁
From Order to Peace
叙事不是线性讲述，而是通过空间和互动自然展开。玩家一开始会觉得房间井然有序——直到发现佛像藏在镜子里、角落闪过暗影、风水暗中冲突。这些荒谬的细节在秩序的缝隙里生长成恐怖。当他们把一切归位后，恐怖消退，安宁浮现。

环境文字碎片（"海纳百川""黄历""空"）在秩序的表面下暗示着失衡。
The narrative unfolds through space and interaction, not linear storytelling. Players first feel the room is orderly — until they discover the Buddha hidden inside a mirror, shadows in corners, feng shui secretly clashing. These absurd details grow into horror within the cracks of order. When everything is restored, horror fades and peace emerges.

Environmental text fragments ("The sea embraces all rivers," "Almanac," "Empty") hint at imbalance beneath the surface of order.
